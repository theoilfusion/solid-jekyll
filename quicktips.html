1. Dilute with a carrier oil

Use of a carrier oil will not dilute the effect of the essential oil but will ensure that oils applied topically are not irritating or overpowering

2. Never use water if you experience irritation

If essential oils get in your eyes or cause irritation to skin, flush and dilute the area with a safe carrier oil to alleviate discomfort.

3. Don't apply essential oils to sensitive areas

We recommend that you avoid applying essential oils to sensitiv areas such as eyes, ears and mucous membranes.
